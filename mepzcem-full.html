About the Chamber

Who we are

MEPZCEM Chamber Inc. ‚Äî the Mactan Export Processing Zone Chamber of Exporters and Manufacturers ‚Äî was organized in 1984 in MEPZ 1 with six founding member companies.

Today, the chamber has grown its membership to 157 companies and support groups, expanding to MEPZ 2, Cebu Light Industrial Park, Cebu Business Park, MRI in Danao City, New Cebu Township One (NCTO) in Naga City, WCIP in Balamban, and PASAR in Isabel, Leyte.

We have not changed our name ‚Äî to remind us of our humble beginning and keep us grounded in our original mission.

The organization aims to promote the general welfare of its member companies, their employees, and the communities where they operate. It is committed to the professional and effective delivery of business assistance services primarily to its members and the Cebu business community.

MEPZCEM advocates for the simplification and decentralization of PEZA, BOC, DENR, and LGU rules and procedures. It has spearheaded various projects on automation, energy and environment conservation, and community relations.

MEPZCEM is the umbrella organization of seven associations in the economic zones, representing professionals in HR, purchasing, traffic, security, accounting, facilities management, and information technology.

Member companies have worked through calamities together ‚Äî typhoons, earthquakes, and the Covid-19 pandemic ‚Äî demonstrating the chamber's spirit of bayanihan and solidarity.

Seven Member Groups

Our Associations

Umbrella Organization

Seven pillars of the MEPZCEM network

MEPZCEM is the umbrella organization of seven specialized professional associations across the economic zones ‚Äî each serving a distinct sector of our member companies' workforce and operations.

HRA
MEPZ Human Resources Association
The HRA brings together HR professionals from MEPZ member companies to share best practices, address workforce challenges, and spearhead industry events like the prestigious Pursuit of Excellence ‚Äî a showcase of HR innovation and industry game changers. Active in sportsfest, inductions, and general membership meetings.
People & Culture
PMA
MEPZ Purchasing Management Association
The PMA unites purchasing, facilities, and maintenance practitioners and contractors across the economic zones. It facilitates knowledge sharing, general membership meetings, and industry training to streamline supply chain operations and procurement excellence within the MEPZ community.
Procurement & Supply Chain
TRA
MEPZ Traffic Association
The Traffic Association addresses the logistical and transportation needs of member companies and their workforce. Working closely with PEZA, bus companies, Lapu-Lapu City, and government agencies, TRA advocates for affordable, safe, and modern transport solutions for workers in the economic zone.
Transportation & Logistics
SSCI
MEZ Security & Safety Council Inc.
SSCI is dedicated to ensuring the safety and security of all personnel and facilities within the Mactan Economic Zones. The council coordinates with local authorities and member companies to implement best-in-class safety standards, emergency response training, and security protocols.
Safety & Security
AMCA
Association of MEPZ Controllers & Accountants
AMCA connects finance professionals ‚Äî controllers, accountants, and financial managers ‚Äî from across MEPZ. The association facilitates professional development, regulatory compliance discussions, and networking among financial stewards of the zone's member companies.
Finance & Accounting
FAMEA
MEZ Facilities, Maintenance & Environmental Association
FAMEA champions energy efficiency, environmental compliance, and facilities excellence. Active in RA 11285 energy conservation compliance, technical sharing sessions, and advocating for sustainable practices across all MEPZ locators ‚Äî from MEZ 1 & 2 to CLIP, SEZ, and PASAR.
Facilities & Environment
MEZ IT
MEZ Information Technology Association
The IT Association drives digital transformation and technology adoption across the economic zones. Supporting MEPZCEM's various automation projects, the association fosters collaboration among IT professionals, facilitates knowledge-sharing on emerging technologies, and helps member companies navigate the digital landscape. Co-hosted officer inductions with MEZ-HRA and leads efforts in IT governance and cybersecurity awareness.
Technology & Innovation
