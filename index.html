About the Chamber

Who we are

MEPZCEM Chamber Inc. ‚Äî the Mactan Export Processing Zone Chamber of Exporters and Manufacturers ‚Äî was organized in 1984 in MEPZ 1 with six founding member companies.

Today, the chamber has grown its membership to 157 companies and support groups, expanding to MEPZ 2, Cebu Light Industrial Park, Cebu Business Park, MRI in Danao City, New Cebu Township One (NCTO) in Naga City, WCIP in Balamban, and PASAR in Isabel, Leyte.

We have not changed our name ‚Äî to remind us of our humble beginning and keep us grounded in our original mission.

The organization promotes the general welfare of its member companies, their employees, and the communities where they operate. It advocates for the simplification and decentralization of PEZA, BOC, DENR, and LGU rules and procedures.

MEPZCEM is the umbrella organization of seven associations representing professionals in HR, purchasing, traffic, security, accounting, facilities management, and information technology.

MEPZCEM has worked with PEZA, NGOs, and government agencies in seeking solutions to support locators ‚Äî cheaper power, adequate water supply, and 24/7 Customs support.

Through its associations, MEPZCEM has been active in relief efforts for victims of natural calamities ‚Äî typhoons, earthquakes, and Covid-19 ‚Äî demonstrating the spirit of bayanihan and solidarity.

Member companies also play hard through Sportsfest, ERT Competition, Fun Run, Golf Tournament, and the Pursuit of Excellence ‚Äî fostering unity and strong relations.

Direction & Purpose

Vision & Mission

Vision
The Leading Chamber of Region VII
MEPZCEM envisions itself to be the leading, dynamic and progressive chamber in Region VII, Philippines ‚Äî committed to the professional and effective delivery of business assistance services primarily to its members and the Cebu business community. It envisions meaningfully contributing to the economic and social well-being of Cebu.
Mission
Serve, Grow, and Unite
To promote programs relevant to the growing needs of the Mactan and Cebu business community. To ensure a mature and professional chamber organization serving as the nerve center in Region VII. To promote harmony among multinational members and advocate adherence to ethical business standards. To institutionalize linkages with local and foreign organizations.

Seven Member Groups

Our Associations

Umbrella Organization

Seven pillars of the MEPZCEM network

MEPZCEM is the umbrella organization of seven specialized professional associations ‚Äî each serving a distinct sector of our member companies' workforce and operations.

HRA
MEPZ Human Resources Association
Brings together HR professionals to share best practices and address workforce challenges. Spearheads the Pursuit of Excellence ‚Äî a flagship showcase of HR innovation. Active in sportsfest, inductions, and general membership meetings.
People & Culture
PMA
MEPZ Purchasing Management Association
Unites purchasing, facilities, and maintenance practitioners across the economic zones. Facilitates knowledge sharing, meetings, and training to streamline supply chain operations and procurement excellence.
Procurement & Supply Chain
TRA
MEPZ Traffic Association
Addresses logistical and transportation needs of member companies and their workforce. Works with PEZA, bus companies, and local government to advocate for affordable, safe, modern transport solutions.
Transportation & Logistics
SSCI
MEZ Security & Safety Council Inc.
Ensures the safety and security of all personnel and facilities within the Mactan Economic Zones. Coordinates with authorities to implement best-in-class safety standards, emergency response training, and security protocols.
Safety & Security
AMCA
Association of MEPZ Controllers & Accountants
Connects finance professionals ‚Äî controllers, accountants, and financial managers ‚Äî from across MEPZ. Facilitates professional development, compliance discussions, and networking among financial stewards of member companies.
Finance & Accounting
FAMEA
MEZ Facilities, Maintenance & Environmental Assn.
Champions energy efficiency, environmental compliance, and facilities excellence. Active in RA 11285 energy conservation compliance and technical sharing sessions advocating for sustainable practices across all MEPZ locators.
Facilities & Environment
MEZ IT
MEZ Information Technology Association
Drives digital transformation and technology adoption across the economic zones. Supports MEPZCEM's automation projects, fosters collaboration among IT professionals, and helps member companies navigate the digital landscape. Leads efforts in IT governance and cybersecurity awareness.
Technology & Innovation
